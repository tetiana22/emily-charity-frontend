About Emily's Inspirational Charity

Emily(Clayton) was a primary school teacher in Nottingham for 11 years. In September 2012 she was diagnosed with cholangiocarcinoma (bile duct cancer) and died on 27th November 2012 aged 33 years. Every teacher hopes that they will make a difference to the young people they are responsible for by their influence, teaching, and the opportunities that they are able to provide - by their inspiration. Emily was inspirational and passionate about ensuring every child she taught had all the opportunities they needed and were not disadvantaged (especially financially) in any way. The charity has been set up by her family, friends, and colleagues in order to fund opportunities for such children to take part in events and activities that will make a difference in their future lives and inspire them to success, fulfillment, achievement, and happiness.

Tea Party Events

Join our tea party events to support our cause. These events are a great way to bring the community together and raise funds for our school trips. The Tea Party takes place every year on the first Sunday of July. At our Tea Party, you can try your luck in the raffle, enjoy delicious cakes and snacks, and relish the delightful company. Children can also participate in a fun treasure hunt!
